# Food Wars

*It might not be the most creative name, but it's descriptive?*

## Story

Fruit zombie apocalypse. Survive as long as you can.

## Aesthetic

Low poly, Food based. Prototype textures for the level initially, maybe a supermarket area later?

PG-13, the game is intended to be incredibly silly. Bright colours to match.

### Level

For now, it'll be in a prototype sandbox esque area. This is purely for easy of level development tbh.

### Enemies

The enemies will consist of various fruits and vegetables. They will attack the player by virtue of running up to them and doing damage at melee range. Enemies will additionally have googly-eyes to help sell the PG-13 aspect by making the enemies significantly more silly.

### Gore

Upon death, the enemies will be gored, gutted or exploded. The idea is to create a visceral death. For example, when an enemy dies due to an explosion, they need to burst apart in a wave of fruity viscera. Being shot regularly should cause bleeding, impalement should pin them against the wall.

## Ammunition Typing

While the weapon itself typically dictates the a intensity and behaviour (Spread, effect damage, area of effect etc.) the ammunition type dictates the overall effect of the weapon. While a railgun may ordinarily pierce an enemy, with ketchup instead of mustard it may give it an explosive warhead.

| Colour (condiment)   | Effect             |
| -------------------- | ------------------ |
| Yellow (Mustard)     | Physical, Piercing |
| Red (Ketchup)        | Explosive          |
| Brown (BBQ)          | Flame              |
| White (Tartar/Ranch) | Freeze             |
| Black (Soy Sauce)    | Black Hole         |
| ??? (Mango Chutney)  | Knockback          |

Ammunition is applied on a **Per-magazine basis**. Reloading before expending all of the coated "bullets" will "clear" the condiment, giving the player a clean slate to work with. A new condiment **cannot** be applied on top of a pre-existing condiment.

## Weapons

Non-Produce items, preferably fast-food.
The weapons have a magazine, which can be depleted. However, unlike a lot of games, the player effectively has infinite ammo. Weapons can be reloaded while the player holds them, but are reloaded passively in their inventory. If the timer completes **before** the player pulls out the weapon again, the weapon is fully reloaded. **Any condiment remaining is cleared.**

The weapons are distinct in characteristics, and aim to feel and play different. This is to promote variety in their playstyle, and allow the player to pick and choose their favourites.

### Hotdog Railgun

Railgun style weapon. High damage, Single shot, long reload. Relatively speaking, the most powerful of all weapons.

### Automatic Muffin Grenade Launcher

High magazine capacity, VERY slow reload, decent rate of fire, high damage.

Self Explanatory. Allows firing of the muffins in an arc.

### French Sub-Fry Gun

French-Fry packages used as a submachine gun. It's built off stacks of discarded french-fry packages, and the foremost is replaced upon consumption.

Fast Fire-rate, low damage hit scan. Yellow tracers will show French fries being fired.

### Croissant Revolver

A croissant held as a pistol.
Allows the player to fire shots in the magazine rapidly, effectively acting as nice backup weapon.
